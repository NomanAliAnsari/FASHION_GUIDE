About Us

Crafting Fashion,
Powering Businesses

Fashion Guide is a trusted garment manufacturer and wholesale supplier based in Jaitpur, Badarpur, New Delhi — serving retailers, resellers, and Meesho sellers pan-India.

We specialise in women's garments, which make up 90% of our product range — covering tops, kurtis, palazzos, trousers, co-ord sets, sweaters and more. The remaining 10% includes men's garments. Our manufacturing unit produces consistent quality at competitive wholesale prices, making us the preferred sourcing partner for boutique owners, Meesho resellers, and bulk traders.
